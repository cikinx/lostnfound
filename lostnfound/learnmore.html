UPTM Lost & Found Portal

Helping you recover your lost belongings easily

How Does It Work?

The UPTM Lost & Found portal is designed to help students and staff report and recover lost items within the university campus.

Whether you lost something or found someone else’s belongings, you can report it through this portal, and we’ll do our best to help you.

Report a Lost Item

How to Use the Portal

Step 1: Create an account or log in with your university credentials.

Step 2: If you’ve lost something, navigate to the "Lost Items" section and fill out a report.

Step 3: If you’ve found something, upload the details and location in the "Upload" section.

Step 4: Check back periodically or receive notifications if your item is found.

High-Valued Items

All the following items will be surrendered to the University Union Department immediately:

    1. Any item presumed to be stolen
    2. Cash
    3. Government Issued Identification, Passports, Visas, etc.
    4. High Value Items. (e.g., jewelry, laptops, debit/credit cards, electronics)
    5. Student ID Cards
    6. Keys

Items Not Accepted

We do not accept the following types of items:

    1. Items that are soiled, unsanitary, or have no apparent value
    2. Food
    3. Pens, pencils, etc.
