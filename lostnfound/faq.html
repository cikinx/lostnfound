FAQs

How do I report a lost item using the Lost and Found system?

To report a lost item, log in to the Lost and Found system using your campus credentials. Navigate to the "Report Lost Item" section and provide a detailed description of the item, including any distinguishing features, the approximate location it was lost, and the date. You can also upload photos if available. Once submitted, you'll be notified if a matching item is found.

If you find a lost item, you can report it anonymously or through your account by visiting the "Report Found Item" section. Provide details such as where and when you found the item, and include a description or photos. The Lost and Found team will handle the rest, and the owner will be notified if it matches their report.

How can I search for items that have been found?

After logging in, go to the "Browse Found Items" section. You can search by item category, location, or keywords. The listings will display detailed descriptions and photos to help you identify your lost item. If you see an item that matches yours, follow the instructions to claim it.

How long are lost items kept in the system?

Items reported in the Lost and Found system are kept for up to 90 days. After this period, unclaimed items may be donated, recycled, or otherwise handled according to campus policy. Be sure to regularly check the system or the physical Lost and Found location if you are missing something.
